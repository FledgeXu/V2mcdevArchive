PLEASE NOTE: THESE ARE ENTIRELY VOLUNTEER POSITIONS

We now have four separate teams handling various tasks:
我们现在有四个相互独立的团队用于处理不同的任务：

Social Media Team
These folks will be moderating our subreddit r/OptiFine (https://www.reddit.com/r/Optifine/) and handling our semi-official Twitter account @ OptiFineNews (https://twitter.com/optifinenews/)
这些人会管理我们的 Reddit 分论坛 r/OptiFine (https://www.reddit.com/r/Optifine/) 以及运营我们半官方的 Twitter 账号 @ OptiFineNews (https://twitter.com/optifinenews/)

Support Team
A new dedicated team to help solve your many issues and questions about OptiFine.
一个新的团队，专门用来帮助你们解决关于 OptiFine 的问题

Bug Hunters
This team will be tasked with testing preview versions of OptiFine, and finding + reporting as many bugs as possible.
这个团队将会用于测试 OptiFine 的预览版本，以及及时寻找并报告 bug。

Discord Moderators
Of course we’re still doing these, especially now. You may have noticed in the past few weeks we’ve been very VERY low on moderators, so now’s a good time to apply for this team.
当然这个团队现在已经存在了。你可能注意到在过去的几周我们只有很少的人在管理，所以现在是申请这个职位的好时机。

As always, our applications will be open for 2 weeks , starting today. If you’re interested in joining any of our teams, you can apply here: https://forms.gle/EXMSN2LaM4gnRXYU9
和往常一样，我们的申请会从现在开始持续开放两个星期。如果你有兴趣加入我们，你可以在这里申请：https://forms.gle/EXMSN2LaM4gnRXYU9
